Privacy Notice

At Medicalink, we take your privacy seriously.

Please review this Privacy Notice to understand how we handle your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you use Medicalink:

  • Name: For user identification.
  • Contact Information: Including email and phone number.
  • Medical History: Information related to your health, entered directly by you or your healthcare provider.

2. Use of Anonymized Data

We anonymize the data entered by users (patients and healthcare providers) and may export this anonymized data for research purposes. This data is anonymized through the use of a HAPI FHIR server on OpenMRS. Anonymized data may be provided to third parties, including for research, potentially on a paid basis. However, personal data will never be shared with third parties for advertising or other commercial purposes.

3. No Use of Cookies or Tracking Technologies

Medicalink does not use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect data about your use of the app.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data indefinitely unless you request its deletion. You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time.

5. User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can access your personal data at any time.
  • Update: You can update or correct your personal data.
  • Delete: You can request the deletion of your personal data from our system.

6. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption and secure AWS servers to ensure the safety of your personal and anonymized data. While we take appropriate measures to secure your data, please note that no online system is entirely secure.

7. International Data Transfers

Anonymized data may be transferred internationally for research purposes. We will ensure that appropriate measures are in place to protect your data during such transfers.

If you have any questions about the Privacy Notice or any other concerns regarding your use of Medicalink, contact us here.
