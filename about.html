About Us

The Medicalink team is composed of dedicated professionals committed to revolutionizing healthcare with innovative digital solutions, ensuring the highest standard of care for your health data.

The Vision

“To transform healthcare in Sri Lanka by replacing outdated paper systems with a secure, innovative digital platform, enabling easy access to critical health information for individuals and healthcare providers.”

The Mission

“To drive the digital transformation of healthcare in Sri Lanka, we aim to create a secure, centralized platform that simplifies access to health records, promotes collaboration among providers, and empowers individuals to manage their health.”
